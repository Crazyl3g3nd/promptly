Promptly

On September 1st, one thousand clients will be discontinued.

We created Promptly to reduce that number. The first text messaging service for human services in the US, Promptly texts recipients about upcoming due dates, terminations, and anything else clients need to know about.

The product of research and collaboration.

Promptly is designed with its users in mind: it is easy to use, convenient, and flexible. It also sends text messages automatically - just load a list of recipients and Promptly will send the text at the right time.
